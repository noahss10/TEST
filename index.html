Who We Are

W.E.B. builds a bridge between the Eastern entertainment market and Western artists — providing end-to-end support from planning and coordination to on-site execution and post-show care. Our work extends beyond the stage, ensuring every collaboration is supported with clarity, care, and consistency — and much more.

Technical Support

We handle sound, lighting, backline, and all technical operations in line with venue specifications.

Artist Hospitality

Comprehensive logistics including transfers, accommodation, and local coordination for comfort and efficiency.

Team Visas

Expert guidance through documentation, timelines, and compliance for a smooth and legal process.

Promotional Campaigns

Localized marketing and communication strategies tailored for each project’s audience and reach.

Show Operations

End-to-end planning, rehearsals, run-of-show management, and contingency support for seamless delivery.

Transparent Communication

Clear, bilingual communication channels and real-time progress updates across all stakeholders.

Our mission is simple and sincere:

To support artists, deliver exceptional live experiences, and create abundant value for investors.

Why Us

The entertainment industry differs across countries — from security regulations to production norms. With years of hands-on international experience, our team provides seamless communication between the artist team and local production. We are your link to ensuring smooth collaboration and the highest production standards.

Our Team

Combining technical expertise, tour management, production coordination, and creative insight, our team — from both China and the United States — works closely together to serve with professionalism, empathy, and flexibility, bridging regional differences with efficiency and understanding.
